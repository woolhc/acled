系统介绍

该系统主要通过对ACLED武装冲突数据进行分析，建立本体模型，使用Karma进行数据集成，将数据集成为RDF文档，并且通过SPARQL进行查询，最后构建了查询应用

ACLED项目介绍

ACLED (Armed Conflict Location & Event Data Project) is the most comprehensive public collection of political violence data for developing states. This data and analysis project produces information on the specific dates and locations of political violence, the types of event, the groups involved, fatalities, and changes in territorial control.

本体

本体的目标是捕获相关领域的知识，提供对该领域知识的共同理解，确定该领域内共同认可的词汇，并从不同层次的形式化模式上给出这些词汇(术语)和词汇间相互关系的明确定义。总的来说，构造本体可以实现某种程度的知识共享和重用，以及提高系统通讯、互操作、可靠性的能力。

ACLED

ACLED (Armed Conflict Location & Event Data Project) is designed for disaggregated conflict analysis and crisis mapping. This dataset codes the locations, dates and types of all reported conflict events in over 50 countries in the developing world. Data are available for public download from the data page.
